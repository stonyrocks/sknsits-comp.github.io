About

The Conference is aimed at providing a base for researchers to bring out their innovation for the betterment of Technology & Society. Two Days International Engineering Sciences Summit to be held at the Sinhgad Institutes, Lonavala during 16-17 March 2017. It will be organized by SKN Sinhgad Institute of Technology & Science; Lonavala under the aegis of Sinhgad Technical Education Society (STES). The platform where all the engineering streams meet together and make conference much more alive. The objective of this conference is to enhance the knowledge of advanced technology in the field of Computer Engineering and Information Technology, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering to motivate young researchers and provide platform to present their findings.



Guidelines for Paper Submission

IESS’17 aims to publish peer reviewed original research result oriented papers in the fields of Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering The full text of paper not exceeding 5000 words, typed in single line spacing of IEEE format in MS World, should be sent to the convener through e-mail at convener.sknsits@sinhgad.edu. Sample paper format is available on our website Maximum four author(s) per paper, Authors registration is compulsory.
